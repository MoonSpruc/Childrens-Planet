Solutions For The Doubts

Frequently Asked Questions

In the FAQ section, you'll find all the information you need to answer the most popular questions to help you make an informed decision about your child's education.
What are the school hours at Children's Planet Academy?
Our school hours are from 8:00 AM to 3:00 PM, Monday to Friday. We also offer extended care options for parents who need early drop-off or late pick-up.
How do you handle food allergies and dietary restrictions?
We understand how important it is to consider children's food allergies and dietary restrictions. At our school, we take all necessary steps to ensure safety by carefully checking the composition of foods and offering alternatives for children with special dietary needs. Parents can be assured that we are attentive to each case and are always ready to provide suitable solutions.
Is there a uniform policy for students?
Our school has a uniform for students, which helps to maintain discipline and creates a level playing field. The uniforms are comfortable and practical and help to create a positive educational atmosphere. We ensure that pupils wear the uniform in accordance with the rules, ensuring comfort and uniformity.
What is the teacher-to-student ratio at Children's Planet Academy?
At Children's Planet Academy we aim to ensure that every child receives the best possible attention and support. To do this, we maintain a low teacher to pupil ratio to create a supportive learning environment. Our groups are small so that teachers can give attention to each child and develop their abilities effectively.
What extracurricular activities are available for students?
At our Children's Planet Academy we offer a variety of extra-curricular activities to help develop children's creative and physical skills. These include: sports clubs, music and dance classes, art studios and hobby clubs such as robotics, chess and environmental science. We believe that these activities help children discover their potential and develop important skills outside the classroom.
How do I apply for admission to Children's Planet Academy?
Applying to Children's Planet Academy is a simple and straightforward process. To apply, you will need to complete the online form on our website or go to the Admissions Office to collect the required documents. We recommend familiarizing yourself with the requirements and application deadlines in advance to guarantee yourself a place. Once you have submitted your application, you will be contacted by a member of our staff for further instructions and clarification.
